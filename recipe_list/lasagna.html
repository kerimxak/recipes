Classic Lasagna Recipe

Prep Time: 20 mins | Cook Time: 1 hr | Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • Meat Sauce: 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 chopped onion, 3 minced garlic cloves, 1 lb ground beef, 28 oz crushed tomatoes, 2 tbsp tomato paste, 1 tsp each of basil, oregano, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  • Cheese Mixture: 2 cups ricotta, 1 cup Parmesan, 1 egg, 2 tbsp parsley (optional).
  • 12 lasagna noodles (cooked), 3 cups mozzarella.

Instructions:

  1. Make Sauce: Sauté onion & garlic, brown beef, add tomatoes, paste, and seasoning. Simmer 20 mins.
  2. Mix Cheese: Combine ricotta, Parmesan, egg, and parsley.
  3. Assemble: Layer sauce, noodles, cheese mix, mozzarella in a 9x13 dish. Repeat.
  4. Bake: Cover with foil, bake at 375°F for 25 mins. Uncover and bake 20 mins more. Cool 10 mins before serving.

Enjoy your lasagna!
