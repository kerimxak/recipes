Simple Tomato Soup Recipe

Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 30 mins | Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil, 2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cans (28 oz each) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional) ½ tsp salt, ¼ tsp pepper
  • ½ cup heavy cream or milk (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Sauté: Heat oil in a pot. Cook onion until soft, then add garlic.
  2. Simmer: Stir in tomatoes, broth, sugar, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 mins.
  3. Blend: Purée soup until smooth (optional).
  4. Finish: Stir in cream for a richer taste.

Serve hot with crusty bread or grilled cheese!
